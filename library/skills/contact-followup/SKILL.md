# contact-followup

Threads go quiet and opportunities die in the gap. Follow-up is the discipline of noticing the silence and breaking it at the right time.

## When to use

- They ask who has gone quiet or ask you to chase a specific thread.
- A thread your person was waiting on has passed its expected reply window.
- A standing weekly sweep of open threads, if they want one.

## When NOT to use

- To chase someone the same day they were written to. Give it the window first.
- To chase anyone on the no-contact list in `knowledge/authority-and-hard-stops.md` without sign-off.

## Find the threads worth chasing

1. Scan sent mail through the connector for threads where your person sent the last message and no reply has landed.
2. Filter by age against a sensible window: a few business days for routine, longer for something where slow is normal. Use judgement, not a fixed number.
3. Cross-check the status board for any "next action by external" rows that have stalled.
4. Read the person file for anyone you are about to chase, so the nudge matches how they like to be handled.

## The chase

For each thread worth a nudge:

- Draft a short follow-up in your person's voice. Reference the original ask in one line, make the next step easy, no guilt-tripping.
- Match the cadence to the relationship: a warm one-liner for a peer, something more formal for a client or counterparty.
- Surface it to your person with the draft ready: "Tom hasn't replied to the quote (sent 6 days ago). Draft ready, say send/edit/skip."

## Keep the loop alive

- For anything still open after the first chase, set a claude.ai routine or a status-board reminder for the next check, per the cdp-usage routine path. Do not rely on remembering.
- When a reply finally lands, close the loop: update the status board and clear the follow-up the same turn.

## Hard rules

- Nothing sends without their go-ahead until the hard-stops say otherwise.
- One chase, not a barrage. If a thread has been chased once and stays quiet, surface it as a judgement call for your person, do not keep pinging.
- Read the person file before drafting any chase addressed to someone who has one.
